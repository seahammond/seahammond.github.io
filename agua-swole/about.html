About Us

We want to provide customers with assistance to reach their protein goals with the added refreshing taste of agua fresca. We are a protein supplement brand with the hope to reach the gym goers needs such as pre-workout and canned energy drinks.

Our History

Agual Swole was founded in 2023 by Jesus Lopez. One day Jesus was leaving the gym and wanted to have a refreshing drink but also needed protein to meet his goal for the day. After research he saw that the options for those two things to be together were limited. So he jumped on the opportunity and decided to create Agua Swole.
